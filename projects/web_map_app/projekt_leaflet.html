This project is about showing hikings, hotels, restaurants and hostels in Sweden.

  • Layers Control will let you how to control your base layers and geojson layers.
  • Preclicked will view coordinates there clicked.
  • Hovering will view hiking's, restaurant's, hotel's and hostel's name.
  • Doubleclick click doubleclick to go back the original map.
  • Buffer will create buffer around hikings.
  • ZoomTo It zooms to layer when you click hikings.
  • Black Hikings.
  • Navy Hotels.
  • Yellow Hostels.
  • Red Restaurants.
